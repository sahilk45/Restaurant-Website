Sri Ramayya Mess and Caterers Governorpet

If you’re looking for homely veg restaurants in Vijayawada, then ‘Sri Ramayya Mess and Caterers’ is the answer. They have 3 meal plans, where you can ask for unlimited servings of each dish. And that, folks, will seem like a blessing when you taste their delicious dal that’ll temporarily transport you back home. Trust us, this place is so homely, it can even give Sooraj Barjatiya films a run for their money.

Must Try: Senaga Podi, Majjiga Charu

Location:Door 20-14-25, Prakasam Road, Governorpet, Vijayawada

Timings: 12PM-4 PM

Cost for Two: 500 INR

GAD Restaurant MG Road

Not every restaurant can serve a heavenly buffet like the GAD restaurant. The spread includes dishes from various cuisines. Our personal favourite is their Tangdi Biryani, made only with leg pieces. Perfect for people who’d fight their way to grab that one precious leg piece. Modern problems require modern recipes, you know.

Must Try: Tangdi Biryani, Chicken 65

Location: 39-1-63, M G Road, Vijayawada 520010 India

Timings: 12PM-10PM

Cost for Two: ₹700/-

Sweet magic Besant Road

Sweet Magic is a famous sweet shop in the city. But don’t get confused, they serve loads and loads of savoury dishes too. In fact, they serve a better Andhra Thali than most fine-dining restaurants in Vijayawada. And they make their own creams too. So yup, they might have a severe identity crisis as a sweet shop, but we’re fine as long as they continue to serve magic on our plates.

Must Try: Onion Uttapam, Andhra Thali

Location:Kasturibai Peta, Beside Marshalls Pet Zones, Moghalrajpuram, Christurajupuram, Vijayawada

Timings:9:30am – 10pm

Cost for Two: ₹500/-

TFL Labbipet

Are you a vegan searching for good restaurants in Vijayawada? Then don’t worry, the good people at TFL have got you covered. Apart from serving vegan food, they also have a menu for those following a Keto Diet. And what’s more? You’ll find delicious greasy food too, in case you’re having a cheat day. Yes, this place really looks out for you like dank memes on a cold gloomy day.

Must Try: Pork Ribs BBQ, Fish Portuguese

Location:36-11-7/A, Santhi Nagar, 1st Lane, Moghalrajupuram, Labbipet, Vijayawada

Timings: 11am – 10:30pm

Cost for Two: ₹400/-

Barbeque Nation Vijayawada

To be honest, we’re no strangers to a Barbeque Nation. But the one in Vijayawada has everything you’re likely to expect and more. Like a few authentic Andhra dishes kept in the main course. So if you’re wondering where bank staff disappear off to during lunch hours, you might want to look for them at Barbecue Nation, Vijayawada.

Must-Try: Pineapple Tandoori, Paan Kulfi

Location: Door 40-1-146A, 3rd Floor, LEPL Centro, M.G.ROAD, Labbipet, Vijayawada

Timings: 12noon – 3:30pm, 6:30pm – 9pm

Cost for Two: 1,200 INR

Babai Hotel Vijayawada

You’ll be surprised to know that the ‘Babai Hotel’ was established in 1942. So if you wanna enjoy a good meal in one of the most famous restaurants in Vijayawada, this is it. And another fun fact, they still serve coffee in steel glasses from a tumbler like they did 79 years ago. These are the kind of stories you come across on those ‘brown history’ pages on Instagram.

Must Try: Idly, Dosa

Location: NRP Road, Gandhi Nagar, Vijayawada

Timings: 12noon – 9:30pm

Cost for Two: 300 INR

RR Durbar Auto Nagar

R.R Durbar is one of the best biryani restaurants in Vijayawada. But their menu is not limited to just biryani items. They serve more than a hundred other savory dishes too. Plus, the place is pretty budget friendly. Perfect for people who are just not done after a single plate of biryani. Which is most people. Because as any true biryani lover will know, there is no such thing as too much biryani.

Must-Try: Chicken Thai Soup, Pepper Chicken

Location: 40-7-3, Jummy Chettu Circle, Moghalrajupuram, Labbipet, Vijayawada

Timings: 11am – 11:30pm

Cost for Two: 600 INR

Royal Spice Hanumanpet

One of the best new restaurants in Vijayawada, the Royal Spice has become quite popular in a short span of time. Mostly because of their breakfast buffets and gigantic thalis. On top of that, their menu features a surprise item every day. So if you’re someone who loves surprises, head there. But if you’re someone like Sheldon Cooper, well then, they do have comfortable chairs.

Must-Try: Rice Combo

Location: Rama Rao Street, Gandhi Nagar, Vijayawada

Timings: 11am – 10pm

Cost for Two: 700 INR

Barbeque Pride Benz Circle

If you’re looking for the best family restaurants in Vijayawada, then Barbeque Pride is a good option. And we’ll be honest, it’s popular for being a slightly cheaper alternative for Barbeque Nation. But when it comes to their quality, it’s pretty much the same. So yup, in India, you can always find a more economical alternative for everything. From electronics to, well, shami kebabs.

Must-Try: Tandoori Pahadi Chicken

Location: 2nd Floor, Trendset Mall, Benz Circle, Labbipet, Vijayawada

Timings: 12noon – 3:30pm, 6:30pm – 10:30pm

Cost for Two: 1,300 INR

Temptations MG Road

Last but definitely not least, we have ‘Temptations’, one of the best veg restaurants in Vijayawada. They’re pretty famous for experimenting with loads of different ice cream flavours on their menu. Some of their sundaes have odd names like ‘Tan-tana-tan’. But once you dig in, your heart will be ‘Won-Wona-Won’. Ok sorry, we’re done here.

Must-Try: Schezwan vada pav, Chola Bhatura

Location: 40-5-2A, Tikkle Road, Labbipet, Vijayawada

Timings:11am – 10:30pm

Cost for Two: ₹700/-
